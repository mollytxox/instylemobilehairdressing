About Us

Hello, I am Johanna Jones, the owner/operator of Instyle Mobile Hairdressing 2003 Ltd.
I have more than 30 years of industry experience, including 5 years with Joico International as a professional hair colour educator.
I am a fully qualified hairdresser, specialising in colouring and cutting and I am passionate about helping you get, and maintain, healthy hair.
Life is busy and we all want to look and feel 100%!
Instyle provides great hair services conveniently to you at your business or residence in a simple and relaxed fashion with a fantastic result! That's what being mobile is all about.
I only use and retail, salon only professional products.
Service area: Upper & Lower Hutt Central.
(Areas outside of the listed service areas will incur a travel charge.)

I look forward to styling your hair soon.
